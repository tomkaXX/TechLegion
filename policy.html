Information Collected for Tracking and Customization (Cookies)

A cookie is a small file that a website transfers to your computer to allow it to remember specific information about your session. Your computer will only share the information in the cookie with the website that provided it. There are two types of cookies: session and persistent. Session cookies last only as long as your web browser is open, while persistent cookies store information on your computer for longer periods.

Techlegion may use session cookies for technical purposes such as enabling better navigation through the site or allowing you to customize your preferences. Persistent cookies may also be used to remember you between visits. You can turn off cookies in your browser; however, this may affect the functioning of some Techlegion websites.
