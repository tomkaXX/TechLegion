About TechLegion

TechLegion is a community-driven Swiss association (Verein) headquartered in Zug. We exist to educate, inspire, and build a legion of innovators shaping the future of technology in Switzerland.

🎯

Our mission

Make world-class technology education, entrepreneurship and space innovation accessible to anyone in Switzerland — regardless of background, nationality, or career stage.

🔭

Our vision

A Switzerland where talented people from every corner of the world can learn, build, and lead the next wave of deep technologies — from AI and space to cybersecurity and clean energy.

Our values

🧠

Curiosity

We learn relentlessly, in public, and we share what we know.

⚙️

Craft

Swiss precision applied to everything we ship — from workshops to hackathons.

🤝

Community

No legion is built alone. Every member matters.

🚪

Openness

Open to newcomers, returners, refugees, and career switchers. Everyone has a seat.

🚀 NASA

Official NASA Space Apps Challenge partner

TechLegion proudly organizes Switzerland's local edition of the NASA Space Apps Challenge — the world's largest annual space hackathon.

Legal structure

TechLegion is a non-profit association (Verein) registered in Zug, Switzerland. Membership is open to anyone who supports our mission and agrees to our statutes and the annual membership fee.
